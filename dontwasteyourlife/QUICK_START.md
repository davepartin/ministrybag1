These questions work beautifully for each chapter:

1. **What is the main point or key idea of this chapter?**
2. **How does this chapter help you understand what it means to not waste your life?**
3. **What specific truth or concept from this chapter struck you most powerfully? Why?**
4. **How does this chapter challenge your current thinking or way of living?**
5. **What is one practical way you can apply this chapter's teaching this week?**

These questions encourage:

- Practical obedience
- Honest self-examination
