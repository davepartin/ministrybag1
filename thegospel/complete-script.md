# Complete Gospel Diagram Script - Updated Version

## Step 1 (Image)
**Explanation:** This short diagram will help explain what John 3:16 means and how a person can become a Christian.

**Verse:** "For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life." — John 3:16

**Footer:** Let's walk through this together.

---

## Step 2 (God)
**Explanation:** In the beginning, there was God…

**Verse:** "In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth." — Genesis 1:1

---

## Step 3 (Body/Spirit)
**Explanation:** And God created man with a body and a spirit.

**Verse:** None

---

## Step 4 (Relationship)
**Explanation:** God loves us and has a wonderful plan for our lives.

**Verse:** Jesus said, "I came that they may have life and have it abundantly." — John 10:10

---

## Step 5 (Sin)
**Explanation:** Sin is anything we think, say, or do that goes against God, and our sin has separated us from God.

**Verse:** "For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." — Romans 3:23

---

## Step 6 (New Path)
**Explanation:** The punishment of sin is spiritual death, leaving us separated from God.

**Verse:** "For the wages of sin is death…" — Romans 6:23

---

## Step 7 (Good Life / Our Attempts)
**Explanation:** These arrows illustrate that we are continually trying to reach God and the abundant life through our own efforts, such as a good life, philosophy, or religion… but we inevitably fail.

**Verse:** None

---

## Step 8 (Cross)
**Explanation:** Our relationship with God can be restored by putting our faith in the death and resurrection of Jesus.

**Verse:** "But God shows his love for us in that while we were still sinners, Christ died for us." — Romans 5:8

---

## Step 9 (Sin on Cross)
**Explanation:** There is a great exchange; Jesus takes our sin and gives us His perfection.

**Verse:** "God made him who had no sin to be sin for us, so that in him we might become the righteousness of God." — 2 Corinthians 5:21

---

## Step 10 (Restored)
**Explanation:** When we receive Jesus as Savior and Lord, our relationship with God is restored and we become His children.

**Verse:** "But to all who did receive him, who believed in his name, he gave the right to become children of God." — John 1:12

---

## Step 11 (Choose)
**Explanation:** Each of us has a choice to receive Jesus or not.

**Verse:** "But to all who did receive him, who believed in his name, he gave the right to become children of God." — John 1:12

---

## Step 12 (Death of Body)
**Explanation:** If we choose to receive Jesus or not, one more thing is sure to happen someday… we will die.

**Verse:** None

---

## Step 13 (Heaven)
**Explanation:** Those who receive Jesus as Savior and Lord will live forever with God in Heaven.

**Verse:** Jesus said, "For this is the will of my Father, that everyone who looks on the Son and believes in him should have eternal life, and I will raise him up on the last day." — John 6:40

---

## Step 14 (Hell)
**Explanation:** Those who reject Jesus remain separated from God forever, and Jesus calls this place hell.

**Verse:** "Whoever believes in the Son has eternal life; whoever does not obey the Son shall not see life, but the wrath of God remains on him." — John 3:36

---

## Step 15 — Response / Invitation

**Explanation:** Consider this diagram now as you read John 3:16 one more time.

**Verse:** "For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life." — John 3:16

**Summary:** God loves you, Jesus came for you, and you can receive eternal life through Him.

### A Simple Prayer to Receive Jesus

If you would like to place your trust in Jesus, you can express your faith to Him in prayer:

"Jesus, I want to know You. Thank You for dying on the cross for my sins. I open my heart and receive You as my Savior and Lord. Forgive my sins and give me eternal life. Help me follow You from this day forward."

### Next Steps

Jesus hears every sincere prayer. If this reflects the desire of your heart, you are now a Christian and a child of God. You should tell a Christian you know, find a healthy church, and be baptized.
