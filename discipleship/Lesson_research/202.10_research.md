# 202.10: Baptism and the Lord's Supper

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 1 Corinthians 11:26
**Description:** The meaning and beauty of the two ordinances as symbols of the gospel - baptism and the Lord's Supper pointing forward to Christ's return.
**Goal:** Understand the meaning and importance of both ordinances and what they declare about the gospel and our future hope.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article VII: Baptism and the Lord's Supper — Both ordinances are symbolic of gospel realities and central to church life.
