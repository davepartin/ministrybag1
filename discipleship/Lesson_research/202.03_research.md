# 202.03: Getting Involved in the Church Family

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 1 Peter 2:9-10
**Description:** Living out the New Testament one another commands - serving, caring, belonging to a spiritual family.
**Goal:** Understand what it means to belong to the body of Christ and move from attending to actively serving.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article VI: The Church — The church is a gathered community of believers called to love one another, serve together, and grow in Christ.
