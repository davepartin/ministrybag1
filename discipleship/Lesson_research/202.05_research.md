# 202.05: The Biblical Storyline

## Overview
**Key Verse:** Revelation 21:5
**Description:** Creation, Fall, Redemption, Restoration - the four-chapter story of the Bible with Jesus at the center.
**Goal:** Trace God's redemptive story from creation through Scripture and see how Jesus is the climax of all history.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article I: The Scriptures — Scripture reveals the unified story of God's redemptive plan.
- Article IX: The Kingdom — God's kingdom is established through Christ and will be consummated at His return.
