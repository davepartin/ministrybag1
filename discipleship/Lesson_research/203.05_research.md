# 203.05: Faith at Work: Integrity, Ambition, and Calling

## Overview
**Key Verse:** Daniel 6:4
**Description:** The real tensions believers face Monday through Friday - integrity under pressure, ambition vs. contentment, rest in hustle culture, calling vs. career.
**Goal:** Navigate the unique tensions of faith in the workplace and develop a biblical framework for integrity and calling in your career.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article XIII: Stewardship — Believers exercise faithful stewardship in their vocations and work.
- Article XV: The Christian and the Social Order — Christians bring Christ's values into every sphere of life, including work.
