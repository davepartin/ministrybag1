## Key Research Books by Series

### 201 — Growing Deep
- J.I. Packer, *Knowing God* — theology of God's character and attributes
- Wayne Grudem, *Systematic Theology* — chapters on God, Man, Salvation
- Timothy Keller, *The Reason for God* — God and suffering
- Baptist Faith and Message 2000 — Articles II, III, IV, V, XIII

### 202 — Growing Up
- Baptist Faith and Message 2000 — Articles I, VI, VII, VIII, XV, XVIII
- Tim Keller, *The Meaning of Marriage* - for 202.02, 202.08
- John Stott, *The Message of the Sermon on the Mount* - for 202.01
- Gordon Fee & Douglas Stuart, *How to Read the Bible for All Its Worth* - for 201.05, 201.06
- BibleProject resources - for 201.07 (Biblical Storyline)

### 203 — Growing Out
- Baptist Faith and Message 2000 — Articles XI, XIV, XV
- Robby Gallaty, *Growing Up* — discipleship multiplication
- Michael Frost & Alan Hirsch, *The Shaping of Things to Come* — missional church
- BLESS Rhythms materials — for 203.02
- Greg Gilbert, *What Is the Gospel?* — for 203.04

### 301 — Leading
- J. Oswald Sanders, *Spiritual Leadership* — all 10 lessons
- Paul David Tripp, *Dangerous Calling* — 301.01, 301.07, 301.09
- J. Robert Clinton, *The Making of a Leader* — 301.01, 301.08, 301.10
- Baptist Faith and Message 2000 — Articles VI, XI, XIV
