# 203.06: Answering Hard Questions

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 1 Peter 3:15
**Description:** Helping believers answer why for the hope within them - engaging culture with grace and truth.
**Goal:** Learn to defend your faith thoughtfully and engage with skeptics and culture with grace and truth.
