# 203.07: Spiritual Warfare

## Overview
**Key Verse:** Ephesians 6:10-12
**Description:** Protecting the mission from the schemes of the enemy through prayer, truth, and community.
**Goal:** Understand the reality of spiritual opposition and learn to stand firm in Christ's victory.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article IX: The Kingdom — Christians recognize that their battle is spiritual and are equipped through Christ for victory.
