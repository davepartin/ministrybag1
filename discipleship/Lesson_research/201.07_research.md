# 201.07: How God Makes Us Right: Justification by Grace

## Overview
**Key Verse:** Ephesians 2:8-9
**Description:** Exploring how we are declared righteous by God's unmerited favor, not by anything we have done.
**Goal:** Grasp that salvation is entirely by God's grace and not earned by works.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article IV: Salvation — Justification is by grace through faith, not by our works or merit.
