# 301.08: Leaders Are Disciplined

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 1 Corinthians 9:24-27
**Description:** Discipline is the unglamorous engine that keeps everything from falling apart over time. Time, consistency, follow-through, long obedience.
**Goal:** Cultivate personal disciplines that sustain effective leadership over the long term.
