# 203.10: Your Calling: Putting It All Together

## Overview
**Key Verse:** Ephesians 4:1
**Description:** Putting it all together - finding your specific team and role in the church and in the mission.
**Goal:** Clarify your personal calling and commit to serving Christ through your church community.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article XI: Evangelism and Missions — Every believer's calling flows from the gospel and finds expression through the church.
- Article VI: The Church — The church is the context where believers discover and exercise their calling in community.

## Recommended Research
- Baptist Faith & Message Articles VI and XI
- Os Guinness, *The Call*
- Frederick Buechner, *Wishful Thinking* on calling

## Scripture to Explore
- Ephesians 4
- 1 Corinthians 12
- Jeremiah 29
