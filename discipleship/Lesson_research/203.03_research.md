# 203.03: Hospitality as Mission

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 1 Peter 4:9-10
**Description:** Using your home and table to welcome strangers into the family of God - hospitality is not entertaining but mission.
**Goal:** See hospitality as a spiritual practice and a powerful way to show Christ's love to others.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article XI: Evangelism and Missions — Gospel witness includes demonstrating Christ's love through hospitality and welcome.
- Article XV: The Christian and the Social Order — Christians show Christ's compassion through acts of hospitality and care.

## Recommended Research
