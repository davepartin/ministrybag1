**The Plant in a Pot / Greenhouse Analogy**
You start plants in a pot. You start plants in a greenhouse. The greenhouse is not the destination — it's the starting place, the protected environment where something new and fragile gets what it needs to take root before it goes out into the world. That's what this course is. 101 is the greenhouse. The goal is not to stay in the pot forever — the goal is to grow roots strong enough to be planted somewhere that matters. Use this in the opening of the lesson or the course introduction. It also ties directly to the "Growing Together" name — orchards don't start as orchards, they start as seedlings that someone tended carefully first.
