# 301.10: Leaders Challenge Others and Build Personal Ministry in Others

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 2 Timothy 2:2
**Description:** The ultimate goal of leadership is to make yourself unnecessary. Spiritual multiplication, passing the baton, sending others.
**Goal:** Commit to developing future leaders and multiplying your impact through investing in others.
