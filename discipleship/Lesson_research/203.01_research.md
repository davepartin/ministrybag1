### Bless

Building on the "tree" analogy, we can now focus on the fruit section. The fruit of a tree is a result of what it absorbs (Be). A mature fruit tree not only produces bountiful harvests for others to enjoy but also generates seeds for the future. The leaves and branches of the tree provide rest and shelter for creatures seeking refuge. A tree that does not produce fruit is dying and unproductive. However, a fruit tree that produces an abundant harvest brings joy to its caretaker.

Blessing others starts in our own home, and overflows into the neighborhood and our workplace.
