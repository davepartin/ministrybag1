**Growing Circle — Three-Color Visual**
The Acts 1:8 concentric circles infographic could use a three-color treatment: green on the bottom (roots, home, where you're planted) and horizon colors — golds or ambers — rising toward the top (Jerusalem → Judea → ends of the earth). The visual idea is that mission grows outward and upward from where you are, like a plant reaching toward light. The colors communicate movement and warmth without being complicated. Consider this as a redesign direction for the existing concentric circles infographic.

**"If Jesus Was Right Next to You" — Character in Mission**
A question worth weaving into the Great Commission lesson: *"If Jesus was right next to you, would you do this? Would you say this? Would you go here?"* This connects to the "who you are at home is who you are" theme from 301.01, but applied to mission: the person you are when you're on mission is the same person you are everywhere else. You can't live a double life and carry the Great Commission with integrity. This question is less a Key Question and more a recurring diagnostic for the whole 203 series.
