# 202.06: How to Study the Bible

## Overview

**Key Verse:** 2 Timothy 2:15
**Description:** Going deeper into inductive Bible study - learning to observe closely and discover what Scripture says for yourself.
**Goal:** Develop confidence and skill in studying the Bible inductively and be equipped to feed yourself from Scripture.
