# 202.06: Living a Life of Worship

## Overview
**Key Verse:** John 4:23-24
**Description:** Worship is gladly reflecting back to God the radiance of His worth. Not just a Sunday service but the posture of your whole life.
**Goal:** Understand worship as the God-centered response of your whole life.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article VIII: The Lord's Day — Worship is central to Christian life, gathering together as a body to honor God.
