# 202.06: How to Study the Bible

## Overview

**Key Verse:** 2 Timothy 2:15
**Description:** Going deeper into inductive Bible study - learning to observe closely and discover what Scripture says for yourself.
**Goal:** Develop confidence and skill in studying the Bible inductively and be equipped to feed yourself from Scripture.

That's when you introduce a deeper inductive method, something like Observation, Interpretation, Application (OIA). That requires the learner to slow down even more, ask questions about context, genre, and original intent, and dig beneath the surface of the text. You can't meaningfully do that without some biblical framework already in place.
