# 202.02: Loving Your Family at Home: Your First Neighbors

## Overview
**Key Verse:** Ephesians 5:25
**Description:** Establishing a gospel-centered home where your family is your first mission field. Covers marriage, singleness, forgiveness in the home.
**Goal:** Understand biblical principles for building a home that reflects Christ's love.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article XVIII: The Family — The family is established by God and the home should reflect gospel values and Christ's sacrificial love.
