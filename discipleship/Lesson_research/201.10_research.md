# 201.10: Giving and Generosity

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 2 Corinthians 9:7
**Description:** Breaking the power of money through tithing and stewardship as an act of worship and trust.
**Goal:** Understand that generosity flows from gratitude and learn to give with joy and faith.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article XIII: Stewardship — Believers are stewards of God's resources and called to give generously as an expression of faith.
