# 201.10: Trusting and Turning: Faith and Repentance

## Overview
**Key Verse:** Hebrews 11:1
**Description:** Our active response to the gospel: turning from sin and trusting in Christ.
**Goal:** Understand that true faith and repentance are inseparable parts of responding to the gospel.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article IV: Salvation — Repentance and faith are the two complementary aspects of our saving response to God's grace.
