# 202.04: Knowing Your Bible: An Overview

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 2 Timothy 3:16-17
**Description:** A guided tour of the Bible - how it is organized, what each section contains, navigating translations.
**Goal:** Understand how the Bible is organized and feel confident navigating its books and translations.
