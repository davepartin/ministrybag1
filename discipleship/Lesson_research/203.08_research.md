# 203.08: How to Disciple Others

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 2 Timothy 2:2
**Description:** The practice of life-on-life investment - how to intentionally walk alongside someone and help them grow in their faith.
**Goal:** Understand the DNA of discipleship and commit to investing your life in multiplying disciples.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article XI: Evangelism and Missions — Discipleship is the central means by which the gospel spreads and believers grow.
- Article XII: Education — The church has responsibility to educate believers in Christian faith and living.
