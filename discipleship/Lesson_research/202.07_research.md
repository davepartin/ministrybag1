# 202.07: Giving & Generosity

## Overview
**Key Verse:** 2 Corinthians 9:7
**Description:** In this lesson you will explore why generosity and tithing flows from gratitude, not obligation, and why giving is the clearest thermometer of where your heart actually is. Knowing that every thing is God's and you are His steward breaks the grip of money on your life and will bring you lasting financial freedom.
**Goal:** Understand that generosity flows from gratitude and learn to give with joy and faith.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article XIII: Stewardship — Believers are stewards of God's resources and called to give generously as an expression of faith.
