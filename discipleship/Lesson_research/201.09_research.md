# 201.09: Suffering and Trials

## Overview
**Key Verse:** James 1:2-4
**Description:** A biblical framework for when life gets hard - trials produce endurance, God is with us in the valley.
**Goal:** Develop a biblical framework for suffering and learn to trust God in seasons of pain.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article V: God's Purpose of Grace — God's sovereignty over all things, including our suffering, assures us of His faithful purpose.
