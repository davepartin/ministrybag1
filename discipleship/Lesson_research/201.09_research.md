# 201.09: Becoming Who You Already Are: Fruit of the Spirit

## Overview
**Key Verse:** Galatians 5:22-23
**Description:** Sanctification is the process, the Fruit of the Spirit is the evidence. God progressively conforms you to Christ's image through the Spirit.
**Goal:** Understand sanctification as God's lifelong work and recognize the fruit of the Spirit as evidence of transformation.

## Baptist Faith & Message 2000
- Article IV: Salvation (sanctification) — Sanctification is the process by which the Holy Spirit progressively transforms believers to be more like Christ.
