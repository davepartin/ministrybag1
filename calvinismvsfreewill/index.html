Core Belief

God is the only active agent in the universe. He decrees even sins for His glory. Humans have no ability to choose good, and the gospel call is only for the 'elect,' not for everyone. The concept of human free will is seen as a total illusion or heresy.

Impact: God's Choices

God is the Author of every detail, including evil (for a greater good). His will is never thwarted. He does not merely 'allow' things; He causes them.

Impact: Man's Choices

Man is a passive instrument. We do exactly what we were programmed to do. We have no 'duty' to believe unless God gives the ability. Resistance is impossible.

The "Daily Life" Mindset

"I don't need to evangelize my neighbor unless I see signs they are elect. If God wants to save them, He will do it with or without me. My depression or sin is also just part of God's decree."
