Planning Your Visit

What to expect this Sunday

If you've never been to Neighborhood Church, you might be wondering what it's really like. Here's what you can expect when you visit us.

Come As You Are

No dress code, no performance, no pretense. Whether you're wearing jeans or a suit, whether you've been following Jesus for decades or are just curious—you're welcome here. We're a gathering of regular people who need grace every single day.

What Happens Sunday

We gather at 10:30 AM for about 75 minutes. You'll find coffee, friendly faces, worship through music, and teaching from the Bible (we use the ESV). Our teaching is conversational and grounded in Scripture—seminary depth with street-level clarity.

Kids Are Welcome

Your children are part of our family too. We have engaging, age-appropriate classes during the sermon where kids learn about Jesus in a safe, fun environment. Parents, you can worship knowing your kids are cared for and learning too.

Meet Real People

After the service, stick around for coffee and conversation. We're not perfect, but we're genuine. You'll meet teachers, nurses, engineers, students, retirees—ordinary people learning to follow Jesus together. No one will corner you or put you on the spot. Just friendly faces and real connections.

Still have questions?

We'd love to hear from you. Reach out and we'll get back to you quickly.

How We Follow Jesus

Be. Bless. Belong.

This is our rhythm—the heartbeat of how we live as followers of Jesus. It's simple, but it shapes everything we do.

Be.

Love God as His Satisfied Children

Before we do anything, we remember who we are. We're children loved by God—not because we've earned it, but because of Jesus. Our identity isn't built on performance. It's built on grace. When we rest in that truth, everything else flows from fullness, not emptiness.

Bless.

Love Others Where We Live, Work, Learn & Play

The gospel isn't something we just believe—it's something we live. We're called to bless the people around us: neighbors, coworkers, classmates, the barista who knows our order. Small acts of kindness, intentional presence, and genuine love are how we reflect Jesus to a watching world.

Belong.

Together We Live as a Welcoming Spiritual Family

We weren't meant to follow Jesus alone. God designed us for community—a place where we're known, loved, challenged, and supported. At Neighborhood Church, you're not just attending. You're belonging. You're family. And family sticks together through all of life's seasons.

Who We Are

A neighborhood church for everyone

Neighborhood Church started with a simple vision: a place where people could encounter Jesus, experience authentic community, and learn to live with purpose. We're not perfect, but we're real. We're a family of believers learning to love God and love our neighbors—together.

Our story is still being written, and we believe you might be part of the next chapter.
