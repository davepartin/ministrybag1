Planning Your Visit

What to expect this Sunday

If you've never been to Neighborhood Church, you might be wondering what it's really like. Here's what you can expect when you visit us.

What Happens on Sunday

Our service starts at 10:30 AM. At our church, you will find good coffee, welcoming people, and modern worship. We teach through books of the Bible, bringing thoughtful biblical depth in a way that connects to everyday life.

Kids Are Welcome

We offer classes from birth to 5 years for the entire service. School-aged kids (K-5th grade) stay with their families during the worship portion and then leave for engaging, age-appropriate Bible teaching during the sermon where they learn about Jesus in a safe, fun environment.

Meet Real People

Come early or stay a little longer for some good conversations with new friends and possibly your neighbors. We're not perfect, but we're genuine. You'll meet teachers, nurses, engineers, students, and retirees. We are ordinary people learning to follow Jesus together. Come meet some friendly faces and build real connections.

Be. Bless. Belong.

This is our rhythm: the heartbeat of how we live as followers of Jesus. It's simple, but it shapes everything we do.

Be.

Love God as His Satisfied Children

Before we do anything, we remember who God is. We're children loved by God, not because we've earned it, but because of Jesus. Our identity isn't built on our performance but on His grace.

Bless.

Love Others Where We Live, Work, Learn & Play

The gospel isn't something we just believe; it's something we live. We're called to bless the people around us: neighbors, coworkers, classmates, the barista who knows our order. Small acts of kindness, intentional presence, and genuine love are how we reflect Jesus to a watching world.

Belong.

Together We Live as a Welcoming Spiritual Family

We weren't meant to follow Jesus alone. God designed us for community where we're known, loved, challenged, and supported. At Neighborhood Church, we don't just attend but belong to the family of God.

Who We Are

A neighborhood church for everyone

Neighborhood Church started with a simple vision: a place where people could encounter Jesus, experience authentic community, and learn to live with purpose together. We're a family of believers learning to love God and love our neighbors together.

Our story is still being written, and we believe you might be part of the next chapter.
