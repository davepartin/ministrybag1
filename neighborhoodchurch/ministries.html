Life Together

Come and Belong

God designed us to grow, serve, and do life together. Whether you're 5 or 85, just exploring faith or have walked with Jesus for decades, our ministries exist to help every person in our church family find connection, purpose, and community.

Neighborhood Kids

Kids Growing Up Together

Our Neighborhood Kids Ministry encourages kids to know they are deeply loved by God, to grow in their love for Him, and to learn how to love others together. In a safe, fun, and age-appropriate environment, kids from nursery through 5th grade experience creative Bible teaching, fun activities, and meaningful relationships with caring leaders and friends.

We believe these early years shape a kid’s identity and faith for a lifetime. Our heart is for kids to be satisfied in who they are in Christ, knowing they are loved by God and their church family. We help kids understand they can be a blessing to others and that they belong in the family of God.

Our volunteers are background-checked, well trained, and passionate about walking alongside kids as they grow into confident followers of Jesus, blessing others where they live, learn, and play, and experiencing true belonging in God’s family.

Youth Group

Students, God loves you!

Middle school and high school are some of the most foundational years and we hope students navigate them with each other and with God! Our Youth Group (NCYG) is a place where teens can ask hard questions, build genuine friendships, and discover what it means to follow Jesus for themselves as we open God's Word together.

Through weekly youth group, Sunday morning Bible study, summer camp trips, and service trips and a whole lot of fun, we hope to help guide the next generation as they grow into who God created them to be.

Contact our Youth Ministry
Young Adults Group

Building Forever Friendships

High school is over, but real life is just beginning. The young adult years — from graduation to your early twenties — are full of huge decisions, big questions, and new freedoms. We don't want you navigating this season alone. Our Young Adults Group is a community where you can find authentic friendships, ask honest questions about faith, and discover what it looks like to follow Jesus in college, career, and everything in between.

Through regular gatherings, game nights, service opportunities, and real conversations, we're building forever friendships rooted in something deeper than shared interests or life stage. We're learning together what it means to love God and love our neighbors as we step into adulthood.

Contact our Young Adults
Women's Ministry

Known, loved, and growing together

Our Women's Ministry exists to help women at every stage of life experience the deep friendship and spiritual growth that comes from doing life alongside other women who love Jesus. Whether you're a new mom, a working professional, an empty-nester, or somewhere in between, there's a seat at the table for you.

Through Bible studies, fellowship events, and intentional discipleship, we create space for women to be real, to be encouraged, and to be transformed by the grace of God.

Men's Ministry

Iron sharpens iron

Our Men's Ministry is built on the conviction that men grow best when they're doing life with other men who are serious about following Jesus. We create space for men to be honest, to be challenged, and to build the kind of friendships that make us better husbands, fathers, friends, and followers of Christ.

From retreats and breakfasts to discipleship groups and service projects, our men are learning to lead with integrity and love with courage. We believe every man needs a band of brothers.

Contact our Men's Ministry
Joy Club

Jesus - Others - You - JOY

Joy Club is our ministry for adults 50 and older in our church family. This group reminds us that fellowship, laughter, and growing in the Lord never gets old. We gather multiple times a month for lunch and game days, plus times of prayer and encouragement that build real friendships.

Whether you are new to Neighborhood Church or have been here for years, Joy Club is a place to belong, be known, and keep walking with Jesus together, sharing wisdom, stories, and joy in every season.

Contact our Joy Club
Serve Teams

Using your gifts to love the neighborhood

At Neighborhood Church, serving isn't just something we do on Sundays — it's part of who we are. Our Serve Teams are the backbone of our church family, making everything from Sunday mornings to community outreach possible. Serving is also one of the fastest ways to build real friendships and feel like you belong.

We have teams for hospitality, setup and teardown, kids' ministry helpers, parking, greeting, audio and visual tech, and community outreach. No matter your skill set or schedule, there's a team that could use you.
