Life Together

There's a place for you here

God designed us to grow, serve, and do life together. Whether you're 5 or 85, just exploring faith or have walked with Jesus for decades, our ministries exist to help every person in our church family find connection, purpose, and community.

Neighborhood Kids

Big faith starts with little people

Our Neighborhood Kids ministry is all about helping children encounter the love of Jesus in an environment that's safe, fun, and age-appropriate. From nursery through 5th grade, your kids will experience creative Bible teaching, worship, and genuine relationships with caring leaders.

We believe that what happens in a child's heart during these years matters for a lifetime. Our volunteers are background-checked, trained, and passionate about pointing the next generation to Jesus.

Youth Group

Real faith for real life

Middle school and high school are some of the most formative years of a person's life, and we don't want students navigating them alone. Our Youth Group is a place where teens can ask hard questions, build genuine friendships, and discover what it means to follow Jesus for themselves.

Through weekly gatherings, retreats, service projects, and a whole lot of fun, we're walking alongside the next generation as they grow into who God created them to be.

Connect Your Student
Women's Ministry

Known, loved, and growing together

Our Women's Ministry exists to help women at every stage of life experience the deep friendship and spiritual growth that comes from doing life alongside other women who love Jesus. Whether you're a new mom, a working professional, an empty-nester, or somewhere in between, there's a seat at the table for you.

Through Bible studies, fellowship events, and intentional discipleship, we create space for women to be real, to be encouraged, and to be transformed by the grace of God.

Men's Ministry

Iron sharpens iron

Our Men's Ministry is built on the conviction that men grow best when they're doing life with other men who are serious about following Jesus. We create space for men to be honest, to be challenged, and to build the kind of friendships that make us better husbands, fathers, friends, and followers of Christ.

From retreats and breakfasts to discipleship groups and service projects, our men are learning to lead with integrity and love with courage. We believe every man needs a band of brothers.

Joy Club

Celebrating the gift of every season

Joy Club is our ministry for the seasoned saints in our church family. This group proves that fellowship, laughter, and growing in the Lord don't have an expiration date. Our seniors gather regularly for meals, outings, Bible study, and the kind of deep, meaningful friendship that only comes from years of walking with Jesus.

Whether it's a lunch outing, a game day, or a time of prayer together, Joy Club is a place where the wisdom of the older generation and the joy of community come together beautifully.

Worship Team

Leading others into the presence of God

Our Worship Team isn't a performance — it's a family of musicians, singers, and tech servants who use their gifts to create an environment where people can encounter the living God. We believe worship is the overflow of hearts that have been captivated by grace, and our team leads from that place every Sunday.

Whether you play an instrument, sing, or serve behind the sound board, there's a place for you on the team. We rehearse weekly and grow together as musicians and worshippers.

Join the Worship Team
Serve Teams

Using your gifts to love the neighborhood

At Neighborhood Church, serving isn't just something we do on Sundays — it's part of who we are. Our Serve Teams are the backbone of our church family, making everything from Sunday mornings to community outreach possible. Serving is also one of the fastest ways to build real friendships and feel like you belong.

We have teams for hospitality, setup and teardown, kids' ministry helpers, parking, greeting, audio and visual tech, and community outreach. No matter your skill set or schedule, there's a team that could use you.
