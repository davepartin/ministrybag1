Life Together

Growing, serving, and playing in the neighborhood

We believe that followers of Jesus are called to live as a spiritual family. Our groups meet in homes throughout the week to share meals, study the Bible, and care for one another.

Home Groups

Casual gatherings in local homes focused on fellowship, prayer, and discussing the weekly sermon. These are the heartbeat of our church family. We eat together, pray together, and share life together.

Discipleship Groups

Specific Men's and Women's groups designed for deeper accountability and sharpening our faith through study and prayer. These smaller cohorts meet to encourage one another in our walk with Christ.

Serve Teams

From Kids Ministry to the Worship Team, serving is one of the best ways to get connected. Join a team to use your gifts and build relationships while serving the body.

Sports Camp 2026

Our biggest neighborhood event of the year! A week of fun, sports, and learning about God's love for kids in our community.

Don't miss out on the best week of the summer. We offer soccer, basketball, cheerleading, and more for kids K-5th grade.
